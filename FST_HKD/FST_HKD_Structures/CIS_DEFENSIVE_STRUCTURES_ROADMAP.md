## RECOMMENDED NEW PRODUCTS

### **CATEGORY 1: PERIMETER WALLS & BARRIERS**

#### 1.1 **CIS Defensive Wall Section (4m, 8m, 12m variants)**
- **Purpose**: Connect barrier points into continuous defensive line
- **Features**:
  - Modular wall panels (4m sections stackable)
  - Flat-top design for droid/trooper positioning
  - Bullet-resistant but not vehicle-proof
  - **Variants**: Unpainted, CIS Markings, Weathered Battle Damage
- **Armor**: 250 per section
- **Model Positioning**: Should interlock seamlessly
- **Use Case**: Base perimeters, checkpoint barriers, convoy staging areas

#### 1.2 **CIS Reinforced Gate/Checkpoint Structure**
- **Purpose**: Controlled entry point with defensive capability
- **Features**:
  - Dual-barrier design (approx 8m wide)
  - Integrated sentry position (raised platform for 1-2 droids/humans)
  - Retractable barrier option (visual model variant)
  - Gun port for defensive positioning
- **Armor**: 500 (Gates/hinges), 300 (Sentry platform)
- **Variants**: Standard, Heavily Damaged, Rusted/Weathered
- **Use Case**: CIS fortified bases, convoy security, territory control

#### 1.3 **CIS Corner/Junction Wall Piece**
- **Purpose**: Create angled defensive positions
- **Features**:
  - 90-degree and 45-degree angle variants
  - L-shaped or T-shaped configurations
  - Maintains cover on both sides
- **Armor**: 300
- **Use Case**: Firebase corners, observation post positioning

### **CATEGORY 2: ELEVATED DEFENSIVE STRUCTURES**

#### 2.1 **CIS Watchtower (Single Platform)**
- **Purpose**: Elevated observation & firing position
- **Features**:
  - 5-6 meter height
  - Platform for 2 droids/humans (prone firing or standing)
  - Ladder/ramp access
  - 360° firing arc
  - Spotlight/searchlight mounting point (visual fixture)
- **Armor**: 400 (Base), 200 (Tower), 100 (Platform)
- **Variants**: 
  - Standard Separatist
  - Partially Damaged (holes in plating)
  - Rusted/Weathered battle-field condition
- **Use Case**: Perimeter surveillance, firebase defense, sniper nests

#### 2.2 **CIS Defensive Bunker Complex (Prefab Set)**
- **Purpose**: Integrated defensive position
- **Features**:
  - Underground/semi-buried design
  - Gun port facing direction (2-3 firing positions)
  - Reinforced roof (resists air support)
  - Internal hatch/access
  - Small ammo storage nook (visual only)
- **Armor**: 600 (Roof/walls), 400 (Doors)
- **Variants**: Combat-worn, Fresh Build, Blast-damaged
- **Use Case**: Field fortifications, last-stand positions, objective markers

#### 2.3 **CIS Anti-Air Emplacement Platform**
- **Purpose**: Elevated firing platform for anti-air droids/teams
- **Features**:
  - Raised platform (3-4m height)
  - Rotating dish mount point (visual fixture, no actual rotation)
  - 180° upper hemisphere firing arc
  - Ammunition rack (visual detail)
- **Armor**: 350 (Platform), 150 (Mounting hardware)
- **Use Case**: Air defense perimeter, Firebase centerpiece

### **CATEGORY 3: SPECIALIZED BARRIER SYSTEMS**

#### 3.1 **CIS Deployable Razor Wire/Electrified Fencing**
- **Purpose**: Lightweight perimeter deterrent
- **Features**:
  - Connectable 3m segments
  - Lower armor (70-100) but good visual deterrent
  - "Electrified" variant with VFX (optional sparks/arcs)
  - Deployable by engineers (flavor/aesthetic)
- **Armor**: 50-100 (Can be cut through)
- **Use Case**: Rapid perimeter setup, prisoner/asset containment

#### 3.2 **CIS Chevron/Caltrops Field (Anti-Infantry)**
- **Purpose**: Ground-level anti-personnel deterrent
- **Features**:
  - Random scatter deployment (2-3m patch)
  - Slow/damage effect (small but cumulative)
  - Difficult to traverse without slowdown
  - Visual: Star-shaped metal obstacles
- **Armor**: 30 (Easy to destroy)
- **Use Case**: Firebase perimeter, defensive minefield aesthetic

#### 3.3 **CIS Concrete Jersey Barriers**
- **Purpose**: Light mobile blocking (convoy or checkpoint)
- **Features**:
  - 3m length sections
  - Stackable height (single or double stack)
  - Minimal armor (150 - breakable)
  - Quick deployment aesthetic
- **Armor**: 150
- **Use Case**: Convoy security, rapid checkpoint setup, mobile defenses

### **CATEGORY 4: ADVANCED DEFENSIVE STRUCTURES**

#### 4.1 **CIS Sensor Array/Radar Dish**
- **Purpose**: Atmospheric defensive structure + visual command centerpiece
- **Features**:
  - Large dish/antenna array on tower (4-6m tall)
  - Non-functional but visually impressive
  - Vulnerable to targeting (lower armor in dish area)
  - Suggests active CIS presence
- **Armor**: 200 (Dish - fragile), 500 (Tower support)
- **Variants**: Active (lights/visual effects), Damaged, Non-operational
- **Use Case**: CIS firebase centerpiece, objective location

#### 4.2 **CIS Repulsor Shield Generator (Aesthetic)**
- **Purpose**: Command structure + morale/immersion element
- **Features**:
  - Tall cylindrical structure (5-7m)
  - Blue glow/energy effect (visual only, no gameplay shield)
  - Central hub with energy conduits
  - Suggests advanced technology
- **Armor**: 350 (Fragile electronics inside)
- **Variants**: Active (glowing), Damaged (sparking), Offline (dark)
- **Use Case**: CIS faction identity, high-value tactical objective

#### 4.3 **CIS Armory/Supply Bunker**
- **Purpose**: Resupply point structure (visual, storage aesthetic)
- **Features**:
  - Reinforced container-like structure
  - Multiple access doors
  - Ammo crate props inside (visual only)
  - Interior visual detail
- **Armor**: 450
- **Use Case**: Supply cache locations, firebase logistics hub

#### 5.1 **CIS Sandbag Emplacement (Small)**

- **Features**:

  - Lower armor (200) - destructible
  - Lightweight feel
- **Armor**: 200
- **Use Case**: Scattered firebase defensive positions, sniper nests

#### 5.2 **CIS Fortified Trench Section (Linear)**
- **Purpose**: Extended infantry defensive line
- **Features**:

  - 4m sections (connectable)
  - Firing platforms along interior
  - Revetments on outer wall
- **Armor**: 300 (Structural), 150 (Firing step)
- **Use Case**: Large defensive perimeter, siege warfare scenarios

#### 5.3 **CIS Rock/Debris Cover (Irregular)**
- **Purpose**: Natural-looking defensive scatter
- **Features**:
  - Irregular large rock formations
  - Multiple size variants
  - Provides medium cover
  - Blends into terrain
- **Armor**: 250-400 (varies by size)
- **Use Case**: Natural firebase positioning, canyon/rocky terrain defense
